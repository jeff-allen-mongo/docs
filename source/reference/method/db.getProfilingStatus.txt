.. method:: db.getProfilingStatus()

   :returns: The current :dbcommand:`profile` level,
             :setting:`~operationProfiling.slowOpThresholdMs` setting,
             and :setting:`~operationProfiling.slowOpSampleRate`
             setting.

             Starting in MongoDB 4.4.2, you can set a
             :ref:`filter <set-profiling-level-options-filter>` to
             control the operations logged by the profiler. If you 
             have a filter set, :method:`db.getProfilingStatus()`
             includes :setting:`~operationProfiling.filter` and
             ``note``.
